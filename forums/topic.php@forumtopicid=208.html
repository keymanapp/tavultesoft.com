# RE: Times New Roman   2008-09-10 07:04:50.730

Tavultesoft Staff
Unfortunately, the Times New Roman font does not include some of the Greek diacritics and so Word tries to be helpful by switching to a font that does support them. I believe Gentium supports all the Greek diacritics and it is easy to read even at small sizes. Gentium can be downloaded from http://scripts.sil.org/gentium_download
# RE: Times New Roman   2009-03-09 05:05:30.727
Amaryllis
Times New Roman in Vista includes greek polytonic characters.
Both Win/XP & Vista also have Tahoma with polytonic greek characters.
Finally all Windows systems after Win/2000 have Palatino Linotype with polytonic characters implemented.
Using any of the above in Word solves your problem.
