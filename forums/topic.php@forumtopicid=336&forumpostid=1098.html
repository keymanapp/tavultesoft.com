Interesting. Found teh answer to my own question. It is an archaic letter known as koppa. [urlhttp://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Koppa_%28letter%29[/url] Click on the link to see the character I was talking about--it is the one on the left side (of the three characters on the upper right corner of that wikipedia page). It apparently had the same sound as the familiar Greek kappa, so the koppa was phased out.
