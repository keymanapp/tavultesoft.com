The Simplified Chinese keyboard is free - you are not required to pay more money to use it. As with all Keyman keyboards, Keyman must be installed to use the keyboard; in the case of this (very complex) keyboard, some of the features needed are not in the Home Edition - hence, the Standard Edition is required.

But once you have a Keyman licence, you do not need to pay any more to use the Simplified Chinese keyboard.
