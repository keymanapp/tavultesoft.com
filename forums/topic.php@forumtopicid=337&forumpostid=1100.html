Keyman Desktop 9 will run on Windows 8 Pro devices but not on Windows 8 RT devices. These are locked down so things such as custom keyboards can not be introduced across the system. We may release an app similar to the iPhone and iPad apps we now have but it will be a low priority due to its small market share and difficulty in covering the development costs.

Keymanweb.com should be working on the Surface RT, however. I will ask the devs to look into that.
