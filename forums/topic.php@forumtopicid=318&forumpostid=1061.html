Hi Pradeeka,

If you upload a keyboard as freeware to the Tavultesoft site, for use with Keyman Desktop, you will not have any details on who is downloading your keyboard. You can get basic download statistics at http://www.tavultesoft.com/account/home/keyboards/statistics.php

If you want to understand and manage your customers, you do need to produce a branded version of your product.
