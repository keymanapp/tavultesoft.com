Hi BGMan,

You receiving this message because you compiled your keyboard with a trial version of Keyman Developer. Keyman Developor can be tested for 30 days: you are not permitted to distribute or use keyboards created with Keyman Developer until you have purchased a licence.

You can purchase a licence online at https://secure.tavultesoft.com/buy/ or contact Tavultesoft Sales sales@tavultesoft.com for other payment options.

Once you have a licenced version of Keyman Developer, simply recompile the keyboard and this message will no longer appear.
