# RE: Microsoft PP 2007 and keyman desktop   2009-06-29 10:46:31.047
Marc Durdin
Tavultesoft Staff
We have received and reviewed your diagnostic report - thank you. This issue most likely relates to the Text Services Framework. PowerPoint 2007 does not have full Text Services Framework support, so you do need to disable the Text Services Framework while you use PowerPoint 2007. There are a couple of ways you can accomplish this:

1. To disable the add-in just while editing, follow the instructions in KMKB0061
2. To uninstall the add-in entirely, follow the instructions in
KMKB0052
