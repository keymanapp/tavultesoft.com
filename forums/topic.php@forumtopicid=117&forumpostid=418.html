Hi Amaryllis,

The diagnostics suggests two issues that may cause Keyman to not function properly:

1) Firstly, and most likely, it says you're running Windows 2000 Service Pack 2. Updating to Service Pack 4 (the latest version) is highly recommended and will likely fix the problems with Keyman Desktop.

2) This may be unrelated and no longer a problem, but you have had three premium-rate dialer trojans on your system. They appear in the Registry startup keys as 'tyng6.EXE', 'Videochat1.EXE', and 'Freevideo1.EXE'. It's possible that they are no longer on your system as they don't appear to be running. It would be worthwhile making sure they're not on the system still, though.

Paul Durdin
