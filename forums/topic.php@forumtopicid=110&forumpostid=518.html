Dear Friend,
As you mentioned that PageMaker 5.0 works with your ANSI fonts perfect & Indesign can't. May be because in pagemaker 5.0 there is a option "Use Typographer Quotes" which always off. And In Indesign CS "Use Typographer Quotes" are always on. That may make some problem with text.

If your problem happens with "quotedbl" {0022}, "minus", "middot" then simply replace those glyph by other.
In the problem with "quotedbl" then just off "Use Typographer Quotes" from Preference menu.

Hope that will works.

With kind regards
Arun Sarkar

P.S.- If your keyboard & font are in ANSI mode then it must works with Indesign.
