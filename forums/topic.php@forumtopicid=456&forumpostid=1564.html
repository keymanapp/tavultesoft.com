Sadly, some Android phones do not support Syllabics, for example most Samsung phones. This is an issue that needs to be taken up with your phone manufacturer -- we are unable to add the font.

With Keyman Pro for Android, you can view webpages within the app and see Syllabics fonts there. However they will not work outside the app. I hope this may be a helpful workaround.
