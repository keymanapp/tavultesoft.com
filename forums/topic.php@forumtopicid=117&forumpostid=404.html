Tavultesoft
Tavultesoft Staff
Hi Amaryllis,

This may be an issue with Norton Personal Firewall after all. Please try the following:

1) Click on the system tray icon for Norton Personal Firewall, and open it.
2) Click on Status and Settings
3) Click on Configure, then Programs.
4) You will see a box with various applications that are listed as having 'Automatic', 'Permit All', or 'Denied' access. Scroll down until you see Keyman Desktop. Change the access to 'Permit All'. Check Keyman Developer as well -- they should be set to 'Permit all'.

You can also try clearing out your IE temporary files:
1) Open Internet Explorer.
2) Go to Tools -> Internet Options.
3) Click 'Delete Files' under 'Temporary Files'.
4) Check 'Delete all offline content' and click OK. This should refresh Keyman's temporary files.


Kind regards,

Paul Durdin
