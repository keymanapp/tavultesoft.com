Hi Kyle,

To use the keyboard, try the following steps:

1) Make sure Keyman is running.
2) Start Word.
3) Left-click on the Keyman icon in the task tray, and choose Classical Greek from the menu.
4) You should now be able to type in Greek in Word.

To return to normal Latin, click on the Keyman icon again and choose 'No Keyman Keyboard'.

Paul Durdin

Hi Kyle,

A couple of other things to check and try:

1) Make sure Word is the active application when you click on the Keyman icon. You can do this just by clicking anywhere in Word before trying to enable the keyboard.

2) What operating system are you using? Keyman 6.2 will not work under 64-bit versions of Windows or Windows Vista.

3) Can you send us a Diagnostic Report from the Support tab in Keyman Configuration please? To do this, open Keyman Configuration, click on the Support tab, and click the Diagnostics button. The report will give us some information on your Keyman Configuration as well as any other software that may be conflicting with Keyman.
