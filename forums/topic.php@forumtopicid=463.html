Jorge, the Greek Polytonic Unicode keyboard lets you type iota subscript with Ctrl+Alt+Shift+I -- this is a little buried on a tablet device and I think that this could be improved for tablets -- the keyboard was designed for desktops originally! However that combination should work.

Full documentation is available at http://help.keyman.com/keyboard/grkpoly2/1.1/grkpoly2.php#osk
