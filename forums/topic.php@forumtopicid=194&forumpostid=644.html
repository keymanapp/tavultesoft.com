Hi Jamil,

You need to have a registered version of Keyman Developer in order to distribute your keyboard layout. You can test Keyman Developer for 30 days - but you are not permitted to distribute keyboard layouts created with the test version. Once you purchase a licence, you can recompile your keyboard layout and it will not show the error messages any longer.

You can purchase a licence online at https://secure.tavultesoft.com/buy/
