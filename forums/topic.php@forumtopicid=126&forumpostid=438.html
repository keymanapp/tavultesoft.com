Tavultesoft Staff
Amaryllis,

Apologies for the delay in responding - I have been out of the office in meetings for the last 4 days. I have been able to track down part of the issue here. Your diagnostic report indicates that you have added the Keyman keyboards through the Control Panel/Regional and Language Options/Languages dialog. Can you try the following for me:

1. Remove the two Greek layouts from Control Panel/Regional and Language Options.
2. Add Greek, but instead of choosing the Keyman keyboards, select US or Greek (either would be fine) for the layout. Do this also for your second Greek keyboard.
3. Click OK and close all the dialogs, then open Keyman Configuration.
4. On the Languages tab, select the Keyman keyboard to associate with each of the Greek language entries.
5. Click OK, then test to see if this fixes the problem.

I am currently investigating the issue with adding Keyman keyboards to the Control Panel/Regional and Language Options/Languages dialog.
